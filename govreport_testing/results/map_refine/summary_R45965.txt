This report provides an overview of the judiciary's FY2020 budget request and the actions taken by Congress in considering the request. It covers the budget submission, hearings, markups, and final enactment of the appropriations. The report includes information on discretionary and mandatory appropriations, as well as ongoing policy issues affecting the judiciary. It also discusses the funding provided in response to the COVID-19 pandemic. The report highlights the importance of the judiciary's budget request and the steps taken by Congress to address it. It provides information on the specific areas of funding and policy issues considered during the appropriations process. The report acknowledges the impact of the pandemic on the judiciary and the funding allocated to address the challenges. It also provides information on the various courts, judicial entities, and judicial services covered by the appropriations. The report concludes by discussing the U.S. Supreme Court, U.S. Courts of Appeals, U.S. Court of Appeals for the Federal Circuit, U.S. Court of International Trade, U.S. District Courts, U.S. Magistrate Judges, U.S. Bankruptcy Courts, U.S. Court of Federal Claims, Probation and Pretrial Services officers, Defender Services, Court Security, and the judiciary's cost containment efforts.
