This report provides an overview of the judiciary's FY2020 budget request and Congress's consideration of the request. It includes information about the major actions involving the budget request, such as the initial submission by the President, hearings held by the House Financial Services and General Government Appropriations Subcommittee, and the passage of appropriations by the House and Senate. The report also provides details about the specific appropriations requested by the judiciary, the courts and judicial entities covered by the appropriations, and ongoing policy issues affecting the judiciary. Additionally, it highlights the hearings held on the Supreme Court's budget request, including discussions about the use of cameras or video recordings in Supreme Court proceedings.
